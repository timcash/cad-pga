Area from Boundary

Boundary sums preserve area while the cone fan changes.

Drag the apex and the polygon vertices. The cone faces change immediately, but the boundary still encodes the same signed area.

\[ \partial K = \sum_i E_i, \qquad A(K) = \frac{1}{2}\left\| o \vee \partial K \right\|_{\infty} \]
