PGA CNC Kernel Simulator

In standard CAM software, spindle rotation and feed-rate translation are handled by separate matrix systems.

Using 3D PGA, the state of the endmill tool is encapsulated in one continuous entity:

Motor = Translator * Rotor

Blue wireframe: the raw stock material.

Red volume: the swept volume of the cut path.

White tool: a spinning four-flute endmill driven by a single PGA motor.

Controls: left-drag rotate, right-drag pan, mouse wheel zoom
